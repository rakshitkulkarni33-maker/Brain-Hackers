Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website, mobile app, or by calling our support team. Simply choose your preferred doctor, select a convenient time slot, and confirm your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and cash payments at our facilities. We also support insurance claims for eligible treatments.

Is my medical information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your medical information. We are HIPAA compliant and follow strict privacy protocols.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 2 hours before the scheduled time through our website, app, or by calling our support team.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services. Call our emergency hotline at 108 for immediate medical assistance, or use our emergency consultation feature for urgent medical advice.
